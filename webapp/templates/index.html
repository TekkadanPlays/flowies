Apps for Workflowy

flowi.es has been discontinued. I know this sucks, and we're very sorry.

What happens is that this was a one-man side-project and I wasn't finding the time or the passion to keep it growing, adding all the apps I once thought I would, and later finding a way to charge for its usage.

Despite all these things, I could have kept it running the way it was, but this, too, was becoming complicated. Backend servers, which previously were running for free, started to be priced, and keeping cron jobs running for updating sites and sending reminders was getting too cumbersome, it required a machine and the perfect configurations on that machine, you know.

In the end we have had at most 35 users. Of these, how many were still using flowi.es to this day? It's a sad world, this world of unknown unused web services.

Goodbye users, and thank you.
